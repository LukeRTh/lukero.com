Who Is Luke?

Luke is from the planet Nemular located in the outer regions of the Glorzapan solar system. He came to Earth originally as a lizard boy but quickly realized that wasn't a thing here so he decided to assume a human form. Ever since, he has been exploring and uncovering the mysteries of Earth and human consciousness all while trying to find rediscover a particular beef jerky brand he once purchased from a gas station in Door, Michigan.
